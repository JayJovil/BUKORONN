ğŸ‘¨â€ğŸ³ How We Make Bukoron

Our special recipe brings together traditional Filipino cooking with a modern coconut twist!

1

Prepare the Filling

Cook the coconut milk in low heat to create the base of our delicious filling.

2

Make the Paste

Add a slurry mixture of flour and water to create a smooth, thick paste consistency.

3

Mix the Ingredients

Add the shredded coconut meat and condensed milk. Mix thoroughly until it becomes a paste-like filling.

4

Wrap It Up

Get your lumpia wrapper and carefully fill it with the coconut mixture, creating the signature triangular shape.

5

Deep Fry to Perfection

Deep fry the turon in hot palm oil and wait until it achieves a crispy golden color.

6

Final Touch & Serve

Take out the turon, coat it with a thin layer of powdered milk, and serve on a fresh banana leaf.
